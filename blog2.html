Database Design Best Practices

Date: February 28, 2024 | Reading time: 10 min

Essential tips for designing efficient database schemas for your Spring Boot applications. Covering normalization, indexing, and relationships.

Step 1: Normalize your tables

Ensure data consistency and reduce redundancy by normalizing tables up to 3NF (Third Normal Form).

Step 2: Define relationships

Use proper relationships: one-to-one, one-to-many, and many-to-many according to the application requirements.

Step 3: Index columns

Create indexes on frequently queried columns to optimize performance and improve query speed.

Step 4: Backup and monitor

Regularly backup your database and monitor performance metrics to detect bottlenecks and optimize queries.
